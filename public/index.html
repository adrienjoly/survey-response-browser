survey response browser

how to use

  1. share the google spreadsheet containing the survey responses with sample-data@survey-response-browser.iam.gserviceaccount.com (read only)
  2. identify the unique identifier of your spreadsheet, from its URL
  3. append that identifier to this URL, in order to display responses
Sample: /1Mfelh98MMmIAqusHi0u2ugoZWGhSjxnMI2GFVoVrRGo (for that spreadsheet)
